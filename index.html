Exposure & shutter
⚠ iOS Safari limitation Manual exposure lock and custom shutter speed require native AVFoundation access, which iOS Safari does not expose to web apps. These controls are silently ignored by WebKit regardless of what the app sends.

Workaround for consistent exposure: Before opening FrameLapse, open the native Camera app, tap and hold on your subject to lock AE/AF, then switch to FrameLapse. On many iOS versions the locked exposure persists briefly while the camera resource is handed off. Results may vary.
